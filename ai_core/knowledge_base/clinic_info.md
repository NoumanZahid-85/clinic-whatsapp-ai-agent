## Appointment Booking
- Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are recommended for specialist visits.
- Appointments can be booked by phone, through our website, or in person at the front desk.
- Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment for paperwork.
- Cancellation policy: Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a $25 cancellation fee.

## Telemedicine Services
- Virtual consultations available for follow-up visits and minor concerns.
- Telemedicine hours: Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
- Cost: $45 per virtual consultation.
- Platform: Secure video link sent via email after booking.

## Pharmacy
- On-site pharmacy located on the ground floor.
- Open during clinic hours.
- Can fill most common prescriptions within 15 minutes.
- Accepts most insurance prescription plans.

## Emergency Protocol
- For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911 immediately.
- The clinic can handle minor emergencies (cuts, sprains, mild allergic reactions).
- For severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, or major trauma, please go to Springfield General Hospital Emergency Room (2 miles away at 1200 Hospital Drive).

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a referral to see a specialist?
A: No, you can book directly with any of our specialists without a referral.

Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?
A: Please bring your photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records.

Q: How long do routine appointments usually take?
A: General physician visits typically take 15-20 minutes. Specialist visits take 20-30 minutes. Please account for additional waiting time for first-time visits.

Q: Do you offer COVID-19 testing?
A: Yes, we offer both rapid antigen tests ($25) and PCR tests ($75). Results for rapid tests are available in 15 minutes; PCR results in 24-48 hours.

Q: Is parking available?
A: Yes, we have a free parking lot for patients with 50 spaces, including 4 handicap-accessible spots.
